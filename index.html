What people will be talking about

Building reliable data platforms

Lessons on pipelines, quality, governance, and scale that survive real production pressure.

Real AI in production

What applied AI looks like after the demo, with tradeoffs, failure modes, and operating reality.

Architecture decisions, bottlenecks, and the patterns teams use when the stakes are high.

Analytics that drive decisions

How strong modeling and clear reporting actually change product, growth, and business outcomes.

Case studies from Israeli companies

Stories from local teams shipping, learning, and iterating in production.
